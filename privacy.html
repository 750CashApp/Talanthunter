How we protect your information

TalentHunter.site is committed to protecting your privacy and using your information responsibly. We collect only the data needed to deliver professional job matching and recruitment services.

Information we collect
  • Contact details including name and email address.
  • Resume information, industry experience, and job preferences.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, search filters, and site interactions.
How we use data
  • To present job opportunities and recruitment matches.
  • To respond to inquiries and deliver support.
  • To improve our website and provide a better user experience.
Cookies and tracking

We use cookies to maintain your preferences, improve site performance, and help us analyze traffic patterns. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Your rights

You can request access to the personal information we hold, ask for corrections, or request that we delete your data. If you have questions, contact us at contact@talenthunter.site.
