Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a job?

Browse listings on the Job List page, select any opening, then use the application instructions on the job detail page to apply directly.

Can employers post job openings?

Yes. Employers can use the contact page to request posting support and receive assistance from our recruitment team.

Is TalentHunter free to use?

TalentHunter provides free browsing for job seekers. Employers should contact us for details about posting and talent acquisition service options.

How can I update my profile information?

Use the contact page to reach our support team, and we’ll guide you through any profile or resume updates.
